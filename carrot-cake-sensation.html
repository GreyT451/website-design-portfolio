The Inheritance of Taste

The Recipe Passed Down.
Perfected Over Time.

“My cakes are birthed from the creativity of my mother. She passed the recipe on to me during my college years, and I have taken it to another level with different varieties.”

What began as a treasured family heritage in Savannah, Georgia, has matured into "Carrot Cake Sensation." Under the meticulous eye of master baker Pernell Johnson, the legendary carrot cake recipe has transformed from a home dessert into a highly decorated culinary sensation.

Today, we craft not just cakes, but memory-makers. Utilizing premium local pecans, fresh organic carrots, and gourmet vanillas, every bite evokes warm nostalgia reimagined for the modern luxury palate.

Pernell Johnson Owner & Culinary Creator

What Makes Us Extraordinary
